About the Department

The Department of Cyber Security, FUTO, is committed to providing world-class education and training in the field of cyber security and information technology. Our programs are designed to equip students with cutting-edge skills in digital forensics, secure software development, ethical hacking, and network defense.

With a dedicated team of faculty members and industry partnerships, we ensure our students are prepared for both academic excellence and real-world problem-solving in the fast-evolving tech landscape.

Our Mission

To train highly skilled graduates in the field of cyber security who can innovate and lead in academia, industry, and government institutions while addressing Nigeria’s and the world’s pressing digital challenges.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading center of excellence in cyber security education and research, fostering innovation, integrity, and global impact.

Stay Connected

At the Department of Cyber Security, FUTO, we are committed to keeping students informed with the latest announcements, academic schedules, and events. Together, we build a vibrant and informed community.
